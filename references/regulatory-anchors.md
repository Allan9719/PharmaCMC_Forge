# Regulatory Anchors For The CMC Skill

This file records the main public guidance anchors behind the skill's project choices. Use these as orientation points, not as legal advice.

## Core Lifecycle Anchors

- ICH Q10 Pharmaceutical Quality System
  - Why it matters: frames lifecycle quality activities across pharmaceutical development, technology transfer, commercial manufacturing, and product discontinuation.
  - Link: https://database.ich.org/sites/default/files/Q10%20Guideline.pdf

- ICH Q12 Pharmaceutical Product Lifecycle Management
  - Why it matters: supports structured change management, established conditions, PACMP logic, and lifecycle-oriented submission planning.
  - Link: https://database.ich.org/sites/default/files/Q12_Guideline_Step4_2019_1119.pdf

- ICH M4Q Common Technical Document - Quality
  - Why it matters: provides the stable structure for quality information in Module 3 and linked quality summaries.
  - Link: https://database.ich.org/sites/default/files/M4Q_R1_Guideline.pdf

- ICH Q2(R2) Validation of Analytical Procedures
  - Why it matters: current ICH anchor for analytical procedure validation in registration applications; use with Q14 for lifecycle-oriented method strategy.
  - Link: https://database.ich.org/sites/default/files/ICH_Q2%28R2%29_Guideline_2023_1130_ErrorCorrection_2025.pdf

- ICH Q14 Analytical Procedure Development
  - Why it matters: provides lifecycle-oriented thinking for analytical procedure design and supporting evidence.
  - Link: https://database.ich.org/sites/default/files/ICH_Q14_Guideline_2023_1116.pdf

- FDA PQ/CMC structured data program
  - Why it matters: reinforces that CMC information is increasingly handled as structured, reviewable data and not only narrative text.
  - Link: https://www.fda.gov/industry/fda-data-standards-advisory-board/pharmaceutical-quality-chemistry-manufacturing-controls-pqcmc

## Change, Validation, And Comparability Anchors

- ICH Q5E Comparability of Biotechnological/Biological Products Subject to Changes in Their Manufacturing Process
  - Why it matters: anchors the comparability logic behind manufacturing changes for biotech and biologic products.
  - Link: https://database.ich.org/sites/default/files/Q5E%20Guideline.pdf

- ICH Q5A(R2) Viral Safety Evaluation of Biotechnology Products Derived from Cell Lines of Human or Animal Origin
  - Why it matters: current viral safety anchor for biotechnology products and relevant cell-substrate risk evaluation.
  - Link: https://database.ich.org/sites/default/files/ICH_Q5A%28R2%29_Guideline_2023_1101.pdf

- FDA Process Validation: General Principles and Practices
  - Why it matters: establishes the lifecycle view of process design, process performance qualification, and continued process verification.
  - Link: https://www.fda.gov/files/drugs/published/Process-Validation--General-Principles-and-Practices.pdf

- FDA Comparability Protocols for Postapproval CMC Changes
  - Why it matters: supports structured planning of post-approval CMC changes and advance agreement concepts.
  - Link: https://www.fda.gov/regulatory-information/search-fda-guidance-documents/comparability-protocols-postapproval-changes-chemistry-manufacturing-and-controls-information-nda

- FDA CMC Postapproval Manufacturing Changes Documented in Annual Reports
  - Why it matters: helps frame reporting-category thinking for certain biological-product manufacturing changes.
  - Link: https://www.fda.gov/regulatory-information/search-fda-guidance-documents/cmc-postapproval-manufacturing-changes-specified-biological-products-be-documented-annual-reports

## Analytical And Stability Anchors

- FDA Analytical Procedures and Methods Validation for Drugs and Biologics
  - Why it matters: clarifies how analytical method development and validation evidence are expected to support product quality.
  - Link: https://www.fda.gov/files/drugs/published/Analytical-Procedures-and-Methods-Validation-for-Drugs-and-Biologics.pdf

- ICH Q1A(R2) Stability Testing of New Drug Substances and Products
  - Why it matters: anchors stability program structure, conditions, and evidence expectations.
  - Link: https://database.ich.org/sites/default/files/Q1A_R2_Guideline.pdf

## NMPA / CDE Anchors (China)

- NMPA 药品注册管理办法 (Drug Registration Regulation)
  - Why it matters: governs the regulatory pathway for drug registration in China, including CMC requirements.
  - Link: https://www.nmpa.gov.cn/yaopin/ypggtg/ypqtgg/20200330164301173.html

- NMPA 药品上市后变更管理办法（试行） / Provisions for Change Management of Post-approval Drugs (Interim)
  - Why it matters: primary China anchor for post-approval change classification and MAH change-management responsibilities.
  - Link: https://english.nmpa.gov.cn/2021-01/13/c_656722.htm

- CDE 生物制品注册分类及申报资料要求
  - Why it matters: specifies Module 3 content requirements for biologic registrations in China.
  - Link: https://www.cde.org.cn/main/news/viewInfoCommon/393a4aaa6f4ed14bb8f984f8ea65bcb3

- NMPA 已上市生物制品变更管理技术指导原则
  - Why it matters: China-specific guidance on post-approval change management for biologics.
  - Link: https://www.nmpa.gov.cn/yaopin/ypggtg/ypqtgg/20210308174201145.html

- NMPA Requirements for Change Items and Application Dossiers of Marketed Biological Products
  - Why it matters: English-accessible anchor for marketed biological product change dossiers.
  - Link: https://english.nmpa.gov.cn/2021-06/18/c_660998.htm

- NMPA Optimizing Registration of Overseas-Manufactured Drugs Transferred to Domestic Manufacturing
  - Why it matters: relevant for inbound localization and overseas marketed products transferred to China manufacturing.
  - Link: https://english.nmpa.gov.cn/2024-04/23/c_1049475.htm

## Additional Quality-System And Record Boundaries

- FDA Part 11, Electronic Records; Electronic Signatures - Scope and Application
  - Why it matters: helps explain why Claude Code should not be treated as the validated system of record or signoff layer.
  - Link: https://www.fda.gov/regulatory-information/search-fda-guidance-documents/part-11-electronic-records-electronic-signatures-scope-and-application

- FDA CGMP regulations overview
  - Why it matters: reminds users that final quality responsibilities remain within formal CGMP systems and roles.
  - Link: https://www.fda.gov/drugs/pharmaceutical-quality-resources/current-good-manufacturing-practice-cgmp-regulations

## Practical Reading Order

If the task is about:
- lifecycle planning: start with Q10 and Q12
- submission structure: add M4Q and PQ/CMC
- process change or transfer: add Q5E and process validation guidance
- analytical lifecycle: add Q14 and FDA analytical methods guidance
- analytical validation: add Q2(R2), Q14, and region-specific method guidance
- stability planning: add Q1A(R2)
- China-specific registration: add NMPA/CDE guidance above
- China/global bridging: add `references/china-global-cmc-operating-model.md`, then relevant ICH/FDA/EMA/NMPA anchors
- record and signoff boundaries: add Part 11 and CGMP overview
